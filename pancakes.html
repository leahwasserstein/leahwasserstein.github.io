Pancakes Recepie

Ingredients:
2 tablespoons of metled butter
1 egg
1 cup of milk
2 tablespoons of sugar
1 tablespoon of olive oil
2 teaspoons of baking powder
1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
1 cup of flour

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine butter, egg, and milk. Whisk together.
Add sugar, oil, baking powder, and vanilla. Mix together.
Slowly mix in flour. You can add more depending on the thickness of the mix.
Put a pan on the stove over high heat. Slowly pour in about 1/4 cup of pancake batter.
If you would like to add toppings such as chocolate chips, bananas, or blueberries, do so here.
Once the top of the pancake starts to bubble, flip it over with a spatula.
Let it cook on the other side for about 1 minute. You can check to see if it is cooked by lifting it slightly with your spatula.
Remove the pancake from the pan and enjoy!
